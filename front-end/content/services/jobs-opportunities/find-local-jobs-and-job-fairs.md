# Find local jobs and job fairs

Verified employment information for Santa Barbara residents will be published
here after coordination with the Municipality of Santa Barbara and the office
responsible for public employment services.

## Information planned for this page

- Current local job vacancies
- Recruitment schedules and job fairs
- Employer requirements and application instructions
- Official venue, dates, and contact details
- Links to verified national employment services

> **Availability notice:** No vacancy shown on this community portal should be
> treated as an official LGU announcement until its source and details have been
> verified. Please contact the municipal government for current opportunities.
